species_spawn_flags

SPECIES_SPAWN_SPECIALis not considered a real character species; used for castes, monkies, etc. this flag will cause the species to skip char-species generation.
SPECIES_SPAWN_CHARACTERSpecies is selectable and visible in chargen. This must be on a species to be roundstart/latejoin.
SPECIES_SPAWN_WHITELISTEDMust be whitelisted to play roundstart/latejoin.
SPECIES_SPAWN_SECRETMust be whitelisted to see this species at all.
SPECIES_SPAWN_RESTRICTEDCannot normally spawn; something must pass in PREFS_COPY_TO_NO_CHECK_SPECIES to spawn checks to use!
SPECIES_SPAWN_NO_FBP_CONSTRUCTFBP of this species can't be made in-game.
SPECIES_SPAWN_NO_FBP_SETUPFBP of this species can't be selected at chargen.
SPECIES_SPAWN_NO_ROBOTIC_ORGANSSpecies cannot start with robotic organs or have them attached.
