HALF_LIFE
(ticks)

Helper to define reaction rate in terms of half-life

Ex. HALF_LIFE(0) -> Reaction completes immediately (default chems) HALF_LIFE(1) -> Half of the reagents react immediately, the rest over the following ticks. HALF_LIFE(2) -> Half of the reagents are consumed after 2 chemistry ticks. HALF_LIFE(3) -> Half of the reagents are consumed after 3 chemistry ticks.
