SHIELD_DAMTYPE_PHYSICALPhysical damage - bullets, meteors, various hand objects - aka. "brute" damtype.
SHIELD_DAMTYPE_EMElectromagnetic damage - Ion weaponry, stun beams, ...
SHIELD_DAMTYPE_HEATHeat damage - Lasers, fire
ENERGY_PER_HPBase amount energy that will be deducted from the generator's internal reserve per 1 HP of damage taken
ENERGY_UPKEEP_PER_TILEBase upkeep per tile protected. Multiplied by various enabled shield modes. Without them the field does literally nothing. 40W
ENERGY_UPKEEP_IDLEBase upkeep when idle; modified by other factors.
MAX_MITIGATION_BASE% Base maximal reachable mitigation.
MAX_MITIGATION_RESEARCH% Added to MAX_MITIGATION_BASE when generator is built using more advanced components. This value is added for each "tier" of used component, ie. basic one has 1, the best one has 5. Actual maximum should be 90% in this case (with best components). Make sure you won't get above 100%!
MITIGATION_HIT_GAINMitigation gain per hit of respective damage type.
MITIGATION_HIT_LOSSMitigation loss per hit. If we get hit once by EM damage type, EM mitigation will grow, while Physical and Heat mitigation values drop.
MITIGATION_LOSS_PASSIVEMitigation of all damage types will drop by this every tick, up to 0.
SHIELD_ABSORBEDThe shield has completely absorbed the hit
SHIELD_BREACHED_MINORThe hit was absorbed, but a small gap will be created in the field (1-3 tiles)
SHIELD_BREACHED_MAJORSame as above, with 2-5 tile gap
SHIELD_BREACHED_CRITICALSame as above, with 4-8 tile gap
SHIELD_BREACHED_FAILURESame as above, with 8-16 tile gap. Occurs when the hit exhausts all remaining shield energy.
SHIELD_OFFThe shield is offline
SHIELD_DISCHARGINGThe shield is shutting down and discharging.
SHIELD_RUNNINGThe shield is running
SHIELD_IDLEThe shield is being kept at an idle state
SHIELD_SPINNING_UPGoing from idle to running
SHIELD_SHUTDOWN_DISPERSION_RATEThe rate at which shield energy disperses when shutdown is initiated.
