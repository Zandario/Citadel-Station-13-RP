riding_handler
/datum/component/riding_handler

Riding handler components

Handles the motion tracking/pixel offsetting/etc of riding

This component takes over after something is buckled to the atom. Before that, riding_filter handles it. main check flags offsets - highly optimized, eat my ass if you can't handle it, we need high performance for /Move()s. component-controlled movemnet operational - general operational - driving
