Vars

allowed_weather_typesAssoc list of weather identifiers, containing the actual weather datum.

our_planetReference to the planet datum that holds this datum.
roundstart_weather_chancesAssoc list of weather identifiers and their odds of being picked to happen at roundstart.

wind_dirThe direction the wind is blowing. Moving against the wind slows you down, while moving with it speeds you up.
wind_speedHow fast or slow a mob can be due to wind acting on them.

Procs

build_forecastCreates a list of future weather shifts, that the planet will undergo at some point in the future. Determining it ahead of time allows for attentive players to plan further ahead, if they can see the forecast.
get_next_weatherUsed to determine what the weather will be soon, in a semi-random fashion. The forecast is made by calling this repeatively, from the bottom (highest index) of the forecast list.
rebuild_forecastGo to the bottom of the list. Get the actual datum and not a string. Get a suitable weather pattern to shift to from this one. Wipes the forecast and regenerates it. Used for when the weather is forcefully changed, such as with admin verbs.
