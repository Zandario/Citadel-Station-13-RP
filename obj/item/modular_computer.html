modular_computer
/obj/item/modular_computer

Damage of the chassis. If the chassis takes too much damage it will break apart. Important hardware (must be installed for computer to work) Optional hardware (improves functionality, but is not critical for computer to work in most cases)

Vars

steel_sheet_costAmount of steel sheets refunded when disassembling an empty frame of this computer.
tesla_linkTesla Link, Allows remote charging from nearest APC.
