proc
Available
(/mob/M, /client/C, harder)

Called to check if this spawnpoint is available for a certain mob and client

@params

  • M - (optional) - the mob being spawned. This is null during latejoins and other instances where the mob is made when there is a spawnpoint, as opposed to roundstart creating all mobs at once
  • C - (optional) - the client of the player
  • harder - ignore checks like prevent_mob_stack
